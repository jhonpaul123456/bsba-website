    Experiences

Holy Cross of Davao College not only prepares you to be competent in many fields of academics, but it also prepares you psychologically for the workforce, resulting in a well-rounded student.     Teachers Perspective

    Your teachers listen to you, encourage you, and laugh with you in every class. When you need extra support or just want to talk, your teachers will be there for you. They'll put their confidence in you to do the right thing, and they'll help you figure it out. Throughout my college experience and reflection on Holy Cross of Davao College, I've come to recognize a number of ideas that have had a significant effect on me, my learning perspectives, and my actual learning. My overall college experience was positive because of the teachers who made a deliberate effort to teach and who taught me to view learning and my job as a learner in the same way.
